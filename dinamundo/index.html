What is it?

Dinamundo was an iOS app that encouraged friends and strangers to break the ice and connect with each other by scanning QR codes on each other's device that then displayed some shared humor.

From the app store description:

Dinamundo delivers jokes and humor to friends getting together in the real world.

We build a custom QR code for everyone who installs the app, designed to look a whole lot prettier than the black and white QR codes you might have seen before.

When you scan a friend’s Dinamundo QR code, or they scan yours, you both get funny fortune cookie messages, written by up and coming stand up comedians in New York and elsewhere. We encourage you to read them out loud to each other (and whoever else is around) for a laugh.

"You will come to a fork in the road. Don’t use it to eat."
"Every day this week will remind you of how great last week was."
"You have great self-awareness. That’s why you have such low self-esteem."

Our jokes are spicy not dirty, and are meant to provoke a laugh not a blush. OK, sometimes a little blush. But we keep it clean.

We ask for your location only while you are actively using our app, aiming to draw lightly on your battery. Apple still reminds you that continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.

Like the best things in life, Dinamundo is free.
