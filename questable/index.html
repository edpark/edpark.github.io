What is Questable?

Questable is designed to “give moments a purpose” by allowing people to earn rewards for charity by completing microtasks on their Android or iOS device on the go.

Companies turn to crowdsourcing services to solve complex problems that require human interaction.
For example, a human can categorize the contents of a tweet more accurately than a computer.

Existing services have failed to attract diverse workforces due to their reliance on small monetary incentives to attract workers. These monetary incentives have successfully attracted bots and spammers, leading to quality control problems.

Questable is a mobile application that allows people to earn money for charities by completing microtasks and surveys on the go. Questable leverages people’s desire to do good, attracting a higher quality and more diverse workforce.
