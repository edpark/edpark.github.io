What is whereu.at?

whereu.at was a service that allowed people to send others their current location in a simple manner. People could use an iOS app, a mobile web application, or a regular desktop web browser to send their location to others and view other's locations sent to them.

From the app store description:

Need to share where you're at with others you know?
Wondering where someone you know is at?

whereu.at lets you do both!

Share your location:
• Pick contacts you know and let them see where you are
• Your location is periodically sent to whereu.at to let your contacts see your up-to-date location
• Location updates are sent even while the app is in the background

Find out where someone is:
• Pick a contact from your address book or enter an email address or mobile number
• whereu.at can send them an email or SMS asking if they'd like to share their location with you
• Get notified when they decide to share where they're at
